Crispy Chicken Burger

INGREDIENTS

  • white bun
  • 2 slice cheddar
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • chicken patty
  • ketchup & mayo

CALORIES

  • Protein 23 g
  • Saturated Fat 7 g
  • Cholesterol 60 mg
  • Calories 500
  • Sugars 8 g
  • Trans Fat 0 g
  • Dietary Fiber 2 g
  • Total Carbohydrate 54 g 18%

INSTRUCTIONS

Take a white bun, make it a little bit toast.
start with putting ketchup & mayo first on the down bun.
place the crispy chicken patty over it.
Then start adding vegies on it, put tomatoes first, then cucumbers, lettuce on top then 2 slices of cheddar.
Then close up the burger with upper side bun & That's it you're done with Delicious Crispy chicken burger. Enjoy!
